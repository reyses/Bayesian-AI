#### Belief Flip Research
- **Hypothesis**: workers should flip when losing + consensus says opposite direction
- Built research workbench: `tools/research_belief_flip.py`
- Replays OOS bar-by-bar with full TBN, checks worker consensus during trades
- **Result: NO SIGNAL.** Workers flip at same rate for losers (5.3/11) vs winners (5.0/11)
  - Best threshold: 44% precision (worse than coin flip)
  - 125 threshold combos swept, none hit 50% precision with 5+ flips
- **Root cause**: workers are price-blind. They don't know entry price, PnL, or stop distance
- **Next**: trade-aware TBN experiment
  - Copied `core/timeframe_belief_network.py` → `tools/research/tbn_trade_aware.py`
  - Workers receive: trade side, PnL ticks, bars held, stop distance
  - When losing: amplify physics signal (velocity/acceleration against trade)
  - When winning: suppress flip signals, trust the position
  - A/B test: baseline (blind) vs trade-aware (sees PnL + velocity)
  - Key metric: SEPARATION — do trade-aware losers flip MORE than winners?
