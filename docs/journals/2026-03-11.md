OOS overlay (session_overlay.py on 855 OOS trades) confirmed:

## Filter Simulation Results (855 OOS Trades)

Tested 13 filter combinations post-hoc on OOS trade log:

| Filter | Trades | WR | Avg PnL | Total |
|--------|--------|-----|---------|-------|
| Baseline (all) | 853 | 95.3% | $9.24 | $7,880 |
| Headroom z_1h >= 2.0 | 42 | 97.6% | $11.35 | $477 |

| Headroom<2 + Fib<=50 | 402 | 96.0% | $10.09 | $4,054 |

Key takeaway: filters provide modest improvement (~$1-2/trade better) but
the real issue is the system captures tiny moves everywhere instead of bigger
moves at structural levels. Need zone mode (chop vs trend) for bigger captures.

## Loss Analysis
- 41 losses examined — most have LOW z_1h (median ~0.7)
- Only 6/41 losses had z_1h > 2.0
- Filters catch ~8-10 of 41 losses at best
- Root cause: losses come from same population as wins, not from identifiable bad zones
