## Blended Pipeline Wired (`python nn_v2/run.py blended`)
Full 8-phase training pipeline matching the process from Apr 7:
1. NMP on IS (no CNN) -> raw trades
2. Regret on raw trades -> optimal direction/exit
3. Train CNN flip (direction predictor)
4. NMP + CNN flip on IS -> flipped trades  
5. Regret on flipped trades -> optimal exit timing (`regret_cnn_flipped.csv`)
6. Train CNN hold (exit timing predictor)
7. Train CNN risk (loser detector)
8. Forward pass IS + OOS (full BlendedEngine, 3 CNNs)

Key detail: Phase 1 uses `use_cnn=False`, Phase 4 uses `use_cnn=True` (loads just-trained flip).
CNN hold reads `regret_cnn_flipped.csv` (Phase 5 output), not the raw regret (Phase 2 output).
Added `_run_blended_forward()` for clean forward pass with all 3 CNNs.

## Exit Physics EDA — Major Discovery
- **100% of long-hold trades oscillate** (cross z=0 at least once)
- **95% cross zero multiple times** — price bounces around the mean
- Current z<0.5 exit kills trades on first touch of mean → 0.2% capture
- Optimal exit is at **oscillation amplitude decay**, not z position

### FADE vs RIDE Exit Modes (from same/flipped split)
| | FADE (same dir) | RIDE (CNN flipped) |
|---|---|---|
| Oscillates | 26% | 16% |
| Never crosses zero | 54% | 69% |
| Exit physics | z decay + oscillation | velocity + vr + reversion |

### 3-Bar Confirmation on All Exits
Single-bar triggers cause noisy exits. 3-bar consecutive confirmation:
- Reduced false p_center exits from 4,237 (-$14.7K) to 111 (-$1.5K)
- Reduced false z<0.5 exits from 5,940 to 287
- Best config: 3-bar on everything, p_center=0.60, total $11,154 IS
