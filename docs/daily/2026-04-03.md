## Breakthrough: 79D + Duration-based NN Architecture

### The Planck Constant
5s is the atomic bar. Every TF is just aggregation of 5s. The entire
trading problem = how many 5s bars of noise to hold through.

### 79D Feature Vector (FEATURE_VECTOR_79D_SPEC.md)
10 features x 6 TFs + 3 helpers x 6 TFs + time_of_day = 79D

**Core 10 per TF**: z_se, dmi_diff, variance_ratio, velocity, acceleration,
vol_rel, bar_range, hurst, reversion_prob, p_at_center

**6 TFs**: 15s (execution), 1m (anchor), 5m, 15m, 1h, 1D

ADX dropped (dmi_diff across TFs IS trend strength). All path geometry,
entropy, PID dropped (derivable or dead signal). Probabilities kept
(unique SFE output, not derivable from z_se alone).

### Strategy Router NN
- Input: 79D state
- Output: direction + hold duration + expected PnL
- Duration options: 5s, 15s, 30s, 1m, 5m, 15m
- Training: every 5s bar gets forward PnL at all durations, label = best
- 218K samples from 311 days clean data
- The NN learns signal half-life: when does the edge decay back to noise?
