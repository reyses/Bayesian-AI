### What Separates Winners from Losers
- **Entry:** wick_ratio (5m, 15m) is the universal quality signal — rejection = confirmed reversion
- **Mid-trade (at center crossing):** bar_range + vol_rel across all TFs = energy. High energy → overshoot. Low energy → stall.
- **Intra-trade:** velocity against for 12+ consecutive 1m bars = energy died (96% WR filter)
- **PnL trajectory:** winners and losers diverge by bar 12 (1 minute)

## Key Insights
1. **Wick rejection is universal** — predicts reversion winners AND overshoot winners
2. **Energy (bar_range + volume) predicts overshoot at center** — not at entry
3. **z_se is bounded by adaptive regression** — 4+ sigma doesn't happen in our measurement. Dollar terms show the real opportunity.
4. **NMP exit is the bottleneck** — entry finds opportunities, exit throws away 97% of the profit
5. **$1 peak trades are trash** — need $5+ minimum to cover costs. 59% of trades reach $5+.
