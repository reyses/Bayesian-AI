## Change Report — SESSION-DAY boundary (DST-aware, CME 17:00 CT)

### What & why
Redefined the trading "day" from UTC-midnight to the CME session boundary
(17:00 America/Chicago reopen, DST-aware). A UTC day cuts through the daily
maintenance halt (16:00-17:00 CT) — it holds the tail of one session + the halt
+ the head of the next, so the gap sits MID-day and each UTC day mixes two
sessions. Session-day = one continuous session (Sun-eve reopen -> next close,
labelled by close date, so "Monday" starts Sunday), halt at the EDGE. User chose
DST-aware (boundary = 22:00 UTC summer / 23:00 UTC winter) over fixed-22.
