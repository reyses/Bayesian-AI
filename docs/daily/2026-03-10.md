## Session Focus
Deep investigation into why 808/997 correct-direction OOS trades give back >90% of gains.

## Key Discovery: 30x Scale Mismatch

Template MFE stats (p75_mfe_ticks) are measured over oracle horizons of 1-30 hours,
but actual trades resolve in 1-5 minutes. This disables the giveback exit protection
via anchor patience (requires 30% of template MFE = hundreds of ticks, unreachable).

- Template p75_mfe median: 486 ticks ($243)
- Actual p75 trade MFE median: 61 ticks ($30)
- Ratio: 12.6% — templates overstate by ~8x
- 455 trades blocked by anchor patience, 260 below giveback minimum

Root cause: K-Means clusters mix patterns from different TFs (15m, 5m, 3m in same
template), so oracle MFE averages multi-hour price swings together.
