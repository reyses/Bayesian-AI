### Daisy-chain oracle (new tool)
User reframe: replace overlapping local-extrema with a SEQUENTIAL
best-extreme-in-window oracle that matches the 1-hour-lapse premise.

Built `tools/regret_daisy_chain_oracle.py`. Logic: start at bar 1 of each
session; in the next W-min window, find the bar with the largest favorable
excursion from entry price (high or low — whichever is bigger); that's the
exit + the next entry. Repeat. Chain resets at session boundaries. Window
configurable (default 60min).

**Full IS 2025 run:** 7,925 sequential trades (vs 11,837 local-extrema
entries — non-overlapping is sparser). LONG 4,005 / SHORT 3,920. MFE mode
$59, median $96, mean $132, total **$1,046,546** (sequential ceiling under
the 1h-budget premise = $4,492/day if every link were tradeable).

Patched `full_window` semantic: changed from "trade used the full window" to
"trade had the full window available." For daisy-chain (which exits early at
the extreme), the former gave 1.8%; the latter gives 86% — the right meaning.
