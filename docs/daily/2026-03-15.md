## Root Cause Analysis: Early Exits
- **SL floor too tight**: `sl_min_ticks=4` ($1.00) — noise on 15s bars. Raised to 12 ($3.00).
- **TF mismatch in bar stats**: Template `avg_mfe_bar`, `p75_mfe_bar`, `shape_delay_bars` are in
  discovery TF units (1m=60s, 5m=300s) but compared against `pos.bars_held` in 15s execution bars.
  A 1m template's "4 bars to MFE" = 4 minutes, but exit engine sees 4×15s = 1 minute → 4x early.
