### Decision — the approach (layered, in order)

### Why (evidence)
- **Burn is day-concentrated, not trade-concentrated**: trade level = broad bleed
  (only 4–5% of causal legs ≤ −$100, ~1% ≤ −$200, worst no-stop trade −$286);
  day level = 8 worst OOS days = 85% of bad-day loss. Bad days iid (lag-1 ≈ 0).
- **Losses are entry-quality**: losers are ~$14-MFE pokes; at +$100 MFE
  P(close<0) ≈ 3%. Trade AGE informative where depth is not (recoverers run ~2×
  longer) — the one underexploited signal. 76% of −$100 legs recover; hold−bail
  positive at every depth → the R-trigger IS the adaptive stop.
