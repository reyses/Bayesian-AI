## How to interpret

- Each segment = the leg between two consecutive opposing peaks (L→H = UP, H→L = DOWN).
- `slope_pts_per_day` = raw rate of change. Sign indicates direction.
- `normalized_slope` = `|slope_pts_per_day| / daily_ATR`. Dimensionless intensity in sigma/day.
  Values > 0.5 = strong drift relative to vol. < 0.1 = essentially flat.
- `n_levels_in_band` = how many human S/R levels lie within the segment's price band.
- `breaks_resistance_up` (UP segments) / `breaks_support_down` (DOWN segments) = did the segment's end price exceed key levels = breakout signal.
