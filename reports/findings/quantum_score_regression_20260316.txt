================================================================
IS data: 4,351 trades (2,859 W / 1,492 L)

FINDING: Quantum score formula (hand-weighted) shows ZERO discrimination.
All q_score quartiles within 2-3% WR of each other.

CONCLUSION:
The quantum fields DO contain information (interactions are physically
meaningful) but they don't discriminate wins from losses at IS trade
entry points. The entries are so noisy (81% reversed/Q1) that even
physics can't separate good from bad within the set of trades taken.

IMPLICATION:
The quantum fields should be a PRE-FILTER (skip low-quality entries)
not a RANKING function (score within existing entries). The lever is
generating FEWER, BETTER entries — not scoring the same 594K candidates
differently.
