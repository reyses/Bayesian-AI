## What NOT to do

- **Do not retrain B1-B8 pickles.** They are read-only artifacts from
  the deliverable. Apply, don't refit.
- **Do not modify files inside `deliverables/composite_zigzag_pipeline/`.**
  Treat that directory as immutable. All new outputs go to
  `DATA/CROSS_DAY/predictions_IS/`.
- **Do not anchor expectations on the deliverable's $927/day headline.**
  The honest floor (post B7-retrain caveat) is ~$600-700/day. DRS lift is
  computed against that floor.
- **Do not pile transformer/LSTM/RL on the same V2 features.** Validated
  Pearson 0.22 information ceiling — capacity doesn't break it.
  (See `feedback_lead_in_pca_rejected.md` and the May 2026 LSTM scenario.)
